## Pathology Quick Reference

> [!abstract] Qì Pathology
> - **Deficiency**
> - **Stagnation** → moving pain
> - **Rebelling**
> - **Sinking**

> [!abstract] Blood Pathology
> - **Deficiency**
> - **Stagnation** → fixed pain, purple blood
> - **Heat** → red skin eruptions, excessive menstruation, bright red blood
> - **Loss** → Qì of spleen holds blood in vessels; stasis can lead to menstrual bleeding
> - Brownish blood during period → typically indicates blood stasis (old blood) or qì/yáng deficiency causing slow movement

> [!abstract] Body Fluids Pathology
> - Deficiency
> - Edema
> - Phlegm
