# 對藥 Duì Yào — Spleen Formula Herb Pairs

> [!info] What is Duì Yào?
> **對藥 Duì Yào** (herb pairs) are two herbs frequently combined together that create synergistic effects greater than either herb alone. Understanding these classical pairings deepens formula comprehension.

---

## Sì Jūn Zǐ Tāng 四君子湯 Pairs

| Pair | Function | Notes |
|------|----------|-------|
| **Rén Shēn 人參 + Bái Zhú 白朮** | Synergistically improve transportive and transformative functions of the Spleen | Strongest Qì tonics for the middle burner |
| **Bái Zhú 白朮 + Fú Líng 茯苓** | Strengthen Spleen and leach dampness | Bái Zhú dries damp; Fú Líng promotes discharge — balanced fluid management |
| **Fú Líng 茯苓 + Zhì Gān Cǎo 炙甘草** | Prevents cloying from tonics | Gān Cǎo is sweet/moistening; Fú Líng is drying — maintains balance |

## Liù Jūn Zǐ Tāng 六君子湯 Pairs

| Pair | Function | Notes |
|------|----------|-------|
| **Bàn Xià 半夏 + Chén Pí 陳皮** | Dry dampness and transform Phlegm | Bàn Xià opens the Qì dynamic; Chén Pí dries dampness and revives the Spleen |
| **Bái Zhú 白朮 + Chén Pí 陳皮** | Regulate Qì and dry dampness | Demonstrated dynamically in Èr Chén Tāng principles |

## Bǔ Zhōng Yì Qì Tāng 補中益氣湯 Pairs

| Pair | Function | Notes |
|------|----------|-------|
| **Shēng Má 升麻 + Chái Hú 柴胡** | The "Raising Pair" 升提藥對 | Small doses (3g each) guide the formula's Qì upward, lifting sunken Yáng; treats organ prolapse |

> [!tip] Clinical Pearl
> Shēng Má enters Yáng Míng; Chái Hú enters Shào Yáng. Together they lift from different channel systems for comprehensive raising action.

## Èr Chén Tāng 二陳湯 Pairs

*Foundation for Liù Jūn Zǐ Tāng and related formulas*

| Pair | Function | Notes |
|------|----------|-------|
| **Bàn Xià 半夏 + Chén Pí 陳皮** | The "Two Aged" 二陳 herbs | Together they dry dampness and transform Phlegm while regulating Qì; both improve with age |
| **Shēng Jiāng 生薑 + Wū Méi 烏梅** | Dispersing/Astringent pair | Shēng Jiāng assists Bàn Xià in transforming Phlegm; Wū Méi prevents over-dispersal and protects Lung Qì |

## Clinical Application Notes

> [!important] Pattern Matching
> When selecting herb pairs, match to the pattern:
>
> | If you see... | Consider pairing... |
> |---------------|---------------------|
> | Qì deficiency only | Rén Shēn + Bái Zhú |
> | Qì deficiency + dampness | Bái Zhú + Fú Líng |
> | Cold in middle jiāo | Gān Jiāng + Bái Zhú |
> | Phlegm accumulation | Bàn Xià + Chén Pí |
> | Qì sinking / prolapse | Shēng Má + Chái Hú |
> | Cough with cold | Wǔ Wèi Zǐ + Gān Jiāng + Xì Xīn |
